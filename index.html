How It Works

Complete Workflow Cycle

From assignment to improvement — see how the entire system works together seamlessly.

1

Teacher Assigns Test

Creates questions, sets deadlines, and shares assignments with students through the platform.

2

Student Completes Assignment

Submits answers, attempts quizzes, and practices problems at their own pace and comfort.

3

Teacher Evaluates & Marks

Scores student responses, provides detailed feedback, and assigns grades for each submission.

4

AI Mentor Analyzes

AI engine analyzes marks, detects weak topics, and generates a personalized improvement plan.

5

Personalized Plan Generated

Practice questions and revision schedules are tailored to each student's weak areas and learning pace.

6

Smart Alerts Sent

Real-time notifications sent to students, teachers, and parents about progress and areas of concern.

∞

Continuous Improvement

Performance improves and the cycle repeats — creating a never-ending loop of growth and learning.
