Teacher Feature Guide

Managing Classrooms

  • Click **My Classrooms** under overview. Enter a name to create a new class.
  • Share the generated 6-character Class Code with students so they can join.

Student Directory & Search

  • Go to the **Students** tab, click a classroom, and view enrolled students.
  • Search & Filter: Use the search bar to find students by Name, Email, or Phone Number (dashes/spaces are auto-normalized!).
  • Click **Stats** to view their academic timelines (showing their quiz scores and difficulty levels securely).
  • Click **Message Parent** to instantly open a chat conversation with their linked parent.

AI Lesson Planner

  • In the student directory, when class history is populated, click **AI Class Insights & Lesson Architect**.
  • The AI will scan the entire classroom's history, find their collective weakest chapters, and generate a customized teaching lesson plan/worksheet.

Assignments Hub & Bulletin Board

  • Click **New Assignment** under the Assignments panel, choose a classroom, and enter questions and answers.
  • Toggle the sub-tabs to view submitted student files/answers, score them, and write feedback comments.
  • Use the **Bulletin Board** sub-tab to post notices or reminders to parents and students.
